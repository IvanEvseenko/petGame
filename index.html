Privacy Policy

Pet Play Fun values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and ensure your trust.

Data Collection

Pet Play Fun does not collect or transmit personal data. All actions — including moving objects and viewing animations — are processed locally on your device.

What This Means

  • No Third Parties: We do not share any data with advertisers or analytics services.

With Pet Play Fun, you can:

  • Enjoy fun moving objects and animations for your pets

  • Use the app offline without sharing any data

Pet Play Fun is suitable for all ages. It does not collect personal data any users.
